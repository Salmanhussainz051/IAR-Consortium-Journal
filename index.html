About Us

International Academic & Research Consortium is a Scientific Research Consortium under the banner of IARCON INTERNATIONAL LLP with the primary goal of promoting the development and strengthening of interfaces between various disciplines in Engineering, General Sciences, Medical and Healthcare, Humanities, Social Science, and Management related fields. IARCON is also involved in knowledge sharing by hosting a variety of conferences, seminars, and workshops on a variety of topics throughout the year. IARC will promote a variety of foreign languages and work with academics, academicians, and scholars to investigate creativity.
